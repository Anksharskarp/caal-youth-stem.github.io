Patriot's Day

Miles and Smiles

The STEM Youth Force team assisted the Lexington Town Celebration Committee in creating and facilitating the 2021 COVID-Conscious Patriots’ Day celebration. This project aimed to reignite town spirit during a particularly gloomy time.

The team set up web pages for the Patriot Points Walk event, where residents would visit historical monuments across Lexington, learn about their significance, and could upload images of their route or of themselves smiling at the monuments.

Despite the tight deadline and demanding technical challenges, the event achieved immense success. Over 200 photos were collected, and the town sprung the life despite the pandemic. Team members had to utilize their time well, facing many frustrating and difficult issues. Ultimately, this improved time management and problem-solving skills and paved the way for future projects.
